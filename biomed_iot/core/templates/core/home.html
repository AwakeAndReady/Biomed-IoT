Get startet with Biomed IoT

Biomed IoT is an open-source IoT platform, designed for collecting and storing sensor data, as well as facilitating automation between devices.

  1. Add and Set Up New IoT Devices: Begin by registering your devices to get MQTT credentials for your device
  2. Automate with Node-RED: Link your sensors and actuators with Node-RED flows to create automation scripts.
  3. Save Data: Securely store your devices' data into the time series database (InfluxDB) within Node-RED.
  4. Visualize Data: Visit the "Visualize" page to visualize and export (csv) your data with Grafana.
  5. Manage Data: Navigate to the "Manage Data" page to selectively delete data.

For additional support, a comprehensive Manual includes FAQs and documentation.
