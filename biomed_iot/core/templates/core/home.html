Get startet with Biomed IoT

Biomed IoT is an open-source IoT platform, designed for collecting and storing sensor data, as well as automation between devices.

  1. Create an account and log in (email verification is required depending on your setup)
  2. Device List: Begin by registering your devices to get MQTT credentials for your device
  3. Automate: Link your sensors and effector devices and store your devices' data into the time series database (InfluxDB) with Node-RED.
  4. "Visualize": Visit the 'Visualize' page to visualize and export (csv) your data with Grafana.
  5. Manage Data: Navigate to the 'Manage Data' page to selectively delete data. More functionality may come.

For additional support, a comprehensive Manual includes FAQs and documentation.
