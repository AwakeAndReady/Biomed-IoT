Get startet with Biomed IoT

  1. Add and Set Up New IoT Devices: Begin by registering your devices to enable monitoring and management.
  2. Automate with Node-RED: Link your sensors and actuators with intuitive Node-RED flows to create smart automation scripts.
  3. Save Data: Securely store your devices' data into the database directly within Node-RED.
  4. Explore Your Data: Navigate to the "Manage Data" page to access your data in a structured tree view or download it for analysis.
  5. Visualize Data: Visit the "Visualize" page to craft dynamic visualizations of your IoT data.

For additional support, our comprehensive Manual includes FAQs and extensive documentation.
