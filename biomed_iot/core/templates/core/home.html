Get startet with Biomed IoT

Biomed IoT is an open-source IoT platform, designed for collecting and storing sensor data, as well as automation between devices.

Best for beginners: The Manual demonstrates the platform with a simple use case.

General Overview
  1. Create an account and log in (email verification is required)
  2. Device List: Begin by registering your IoT devices to get MQTT credentials for your device.
  3. Message and topic structure: Learn about data format and topic structure
  4. Code Examples: Copy and paste code templates for your IoT devices
  5. Automate: Link your sensors and actuators and store your devices' data into the time series database (InfluxDB) with Node-RED.
  6. "Visualize": Visit the 'Visualize' page to visualize and export (csv) your data with Grafana.
  7. Delete Data: Navigate to the 'Delete Data' page to selectively delete data. More functionality may come.
